Location: Breh'inni 6
Date: 207.10.15

An RNS collection squadron has tracked a strange salvage target into the outer debris fields of Breh'inni 6.

The object is moving under its own power, broadcasting no registration, and ignoring every warning burst sent across the local channels. It might be a derelict. It might be bait. Either way, command wants it disabled before it slips back into the junk belts.

Bring it down intact if you can. Keep RNS Dreamboat alive if you cannot.
